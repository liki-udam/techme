How much does a website cost?

Your website venture will be special to you and your business, so we’ll walk you through a scope to prepare to discover out what your needs are before we quote for the project. Whether you’re searching for $500 site or a $10000 site, our TechMe team can handle it.

How long does a website take to build?

A small-scale website ordinarily takes 6 to 8 weeks whereas bigger ventures can be anyplace from 12 to 24 weeks. We will give you with a full guide with points of reference and timeframes for your entire project prior to start, so you won’t have any surprises.

What website platforms do you build on?

Our main platforms are Shopify and WordPress.

While those are our preferred platforms, we are still able to help you with your existing website if it’s built in another popular platforms.

Do you offer website hosting?

Yes. We use Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and GoDaddy as our hosting solutions.

Will my website work on smart phones and tablets as well?

Yes, we will build your website to be responsive for iPads, iPhones, and Android devices to ensure that your website looks good and is easy to navigate on any device.

Can you design my site to help me rank higher on Google?

We follow the best practice methods recommended by Google. We will do an initial optimisation with SEO on all our websites to give you the best starting point for driving new business through your new site.
